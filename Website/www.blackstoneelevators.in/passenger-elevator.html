A machine room-less (MRL) lift is an elevator that eliminates the need for a separate machine room by housing its machinery, such as the motor, directly within the elevator shaft. This space-saving design makes them a practical and economical solution for new construction and existing buildings, especially for low- to mid-rise applications, as it frees up valuable floor space. They are often equipped with gearless traction motors, which makes them energy-efficient and provides smooth operation.
