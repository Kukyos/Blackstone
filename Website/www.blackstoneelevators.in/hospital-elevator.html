A hospital lift, or stretcher elevator, is a specialized elevator designed for the safe, smooth, and efficient vertical transportation of patients on stretchers or wheelchairs, as well as medical staff and equipment. These elevators have larger cabins, wider doors, and features like low-jerk acceleration and anti-slip flooring for patient comfort and safety, along with backup power, emergency alarms, and communication systems for reliability. They are essential for quick and secure patient movement, especially in emergencies, and help reduce physical strain on caregivers and prevent injuries.
