## 8. Safety features (advertised on every lift)

Standard on every installation per current aeplica copy:
- Door interlocks (operation blocked when doors aren't sealed)
- Overload sensors with audio-visual alerts
- Auto-rescue device (ARD) — lowers cabin on power loss
- Battery backup + emergency lowering
- Intercom to ground floor
- Compliance: IS 14665 (Indian Standard) referencing EN 81 norms
